Navient Solutions LLC Complaints

Navient is one of the most well known federal student loan servicers, however many lawsuits have been filed against them. These lawsuits go all the way back to 2017, as states complained that Navient failed to act in the best interest of their costumers. Over the years, the lawsuits have piled up, yet the repruccsions were small. Anyone who recieved false information about their loans did not recieve any sort of compensation. Thousands of complaints have been individually filed by each state, and this data is documented in the graphs below.
